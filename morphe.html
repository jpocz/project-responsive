Morphe Brushes
Anastasia Beverly Hills

Morphe Brushes is one of my favorite makeup brands. They are the first brand I discovered when I first started getting into makeup. I have every Morphe brush they have created. They are so great for blending and so easy to clean. I recommend them to everyone.

Morphe has had many collaborations over the years. Two of my favorites being Jaclyn Hill and James Charles. They both are two people I have really looked up to in my makeup career. James Charles' palette was restocked and sold out for almost a whole year.

The past few years, I have asked for a spcific Morphe palette for Christmas or for my birthday. They just came out with a new holiday palette that I kind of have my eye on. It is very pretty.

Julia Poczkalski - November 22nd, 2020
