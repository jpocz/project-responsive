Colourpop Cosmetics is most definiely one of my favorite makeup brands to use on myself and on my clients. They have so many products for every skin type and they cater to every skin tone as well which is very important.

I also have the honor of being in their ambassador program where I get a few PR packages every month from them to try out, post and talk about with my followers. I just got one of their newest collections the other day, which I am very excited about!

Colourpop Cosmetics as many, many palettes to choose from including full colorful palettes and neutral palettes. They also have eyeshadow singles and build your own palette options. They just released their holiday gift sets which include their sister brands Sol Body which is a body care line and Fourth Ray Beauty which is a skin care line.
