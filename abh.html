Anastasia Beverly Hills

Anastasia Beverly Hills is another one of my favorite makeup brands. They are on the high end of the scale rather than Colourpop and Morphe which are more affordable.

Anastasia Beverly Hills started off as just an eyebrow company and since the 90's they have expanded to eyeshadow palettes, lip glosses and lip sticks and more.

My favorite products from them are their eyeshadows and eyebrow products. Their eyeshadows blend like a dream and are so pigmented. Their eyebrow products are the best of the best. As being specialized in eyebrows,I believe they have the best products for them.

Julia Poczkalski - November 22nd, 2020
